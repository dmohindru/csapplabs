Argument passing conventions
----------------------------
Argument No.	Register
	1			%rdi
	2			%rsi
	3			%rdx
	4			%rcx
	5			%r8
	6			%r9
	
Callee saved register
---------------------
%r12, %r13, %r14, %rbx, %rbp

Caller saved register
---------------------
All other register other than %rsp are
caller saved.
